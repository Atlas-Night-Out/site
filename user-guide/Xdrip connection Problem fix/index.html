Xdrip connection Problem fix




1. Make sure you are up to date on xdrip. You can this by Following the links below
2. If you are working on a PC Download latest version of Xdrip from here Click Here or goto https://github.com/NightscoutFoundation/xDrip/releases 3. If you are working on a Mobile - Go to Chrome on your mobile & Download the latest version of Xdrip on to your Android Mobile Device Click Here to download or go to https://jamorham.github.io/#xdrip-plus in your broswer.
4. First try un-pairing your Bluetooth on mobile if itâ€™s still paired to it.


5. Then go to your system status in xdrip and forget device and restart the Collector


6. Now Re-boot your mobile
7. After re-booting, go back to your System Status page and swipe left to your Classic Status Page
and wait for it to re-pair to your Bluetooth mobile again.
8.
This can take a while be patient, but stay on the Classic page for it to do the process of connecting to your device.
9. Once connected to your Bluetooth you will need to re-boot again
10. Hope you find this video helpfull and fixes your issues.
